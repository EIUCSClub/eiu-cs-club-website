About the CS Club

The CS Club at EIU was formed in Spring 2021 with the intent to provide students with academic, social, and career benefits. The club has evolved from presentations in Discord (due to COVID-19) to in-person events and activities, and we hope to continue to offer exciting and information events as we continue to develop.


Questions? Email us at csclub@eiu.edu, or reach out to a leader directly below!
